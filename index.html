Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I contact Expedia Customer Service?
A: You can reach Expedia Customer Service by calling 1-866-294-6025 for quick support.

Q: How can I cancel my booking on Expedia?
A: Log in to your account, go to "My Trips," select the booking, and click on "Cancel." You can also call 1-866-294-6025 for help.

Q: Can I change my flight or hotel reservations?
A: Yes, visit "My Trips" on Expedia.com or contact 1-866-294-6025 for assistance with changes.

Q: What if I didn’t receive my confirmation email?
A: Check your spam/junk folder. If not found, call 1-866-294-6025 for help retrieving your booking details.

Q: How do I request a refund?
A: Refund eligibility depends on the booking terms. Visit your itinerary in "My Trips" or contact 1-866-294-6025.

Q: What should I do if there’s a problem with my hotel check-in?
A: Call Expedia at 1-866-294-6025, and they will assist you in resolving the issue with the hotel.

Q: How do I use a coupon or promo code on Expedia?
A: Enter the promo code during checkout. If you face issues, contact 1-866-294-6025.

Q: Can I book travel for someone else?
A: Yes, you can book for someone else. For any booking-related questions, call 1-866-294-6025.

Q: How do I view my itinerary?
A: Sign in to your Expedia account and click on "My Trips," or call 1-866-294-6025 for help.

Q: What payment methods does Expedia accept?
A: Expedia accepts major credit/debit cards and some alternative payment options. For payment issues, call 1-866-294-6025.
